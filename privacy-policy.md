## PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated: November 2025**

### 1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Yearly ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Bible reading application.

### 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

#### 2.1 Information You Provide

- Reading progress and completion status
- Notes you create for Bible passages
- App settings and preferences (translation choice, notification settings, start date)
- Subscription status and purchase history (processed by Apple)

#### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- App usage statistics (locally stored only)
- Device type and iOS version (for app compatibility)
- Notification preferences

### 3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Save your reading progress and preferences
- Sync your data across your devices (if you enable iCloud sync)
- Send reading reminders (if you enable notifications)
- Provide customer support
- Improve the App experience

### 4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

#### 4.1 Local Storage

- All your data is stored locally on your device by default
- We use Apple's standard secure storage mechanisms

#### 4.2 iCloud Sync (Premium Feature)

- If you enable iCloud sync, your settings, progress, and notes are stored in your personal iCloud account
- This data is encrypted and only accessible to you
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- Your data never leaves Apple's ecosystem

#### 4.3 No Third-Party Storage

- We do not store your data on any servers we control
- We do not use third-party cloud services
- All data storage uses Apple's infrastructure

### 5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

#### 5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

#### 5.2 Apple Services

- Subscription payments are processed by Apple through the App Store
- Apple's privacy policy governs their handling of payment information
- We only receive confirmation of subscription status, not payment details

#### 5.3 No Analytics or Tracking

- We do not use third-party analytics services
- We do not track your behavior across apps or websites
- We do not collect advertising identifiers

#### 5.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

### 6. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

#### 6.1 Access and Control

- You have full access to all your data within the App
- You can view, edit, or delete your notes at any time
- You can reset your reading progress at any time
- You can export your notes through the App

#### 6.2 Data Deletion

- Deleting the App removes all locally stored data
- Deleting your iCloud data removes synced information
- We cannot recover deleted data

### 7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents and guardians are responsible for monitoring their children's use of the App.

### 8. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

The App does not contain links to external websites or third-party services.

### 9. PUSH NOTIFICATIONS

- You can enable optional reading reminders
- Notifications are generated locally on your device
- We do not send marketing notifications
- You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings

### 10. CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- Know what personal information is collected
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)

### 11. INTERNATIONAL USERS

The App is designed for users worldwide. By using the App, you consent to the transfer and processing of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

### 12. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

### 13. DATA RETENTION

- We retain your data only as long as you use the App
- Uninstalling the App deletes local data
- iCloud data persists in your iCloud account until you delete it

### 14. CONTACT US

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your privacy, please contact us through the App Store.

### 15. YOUR CONSENT

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
