Yearly for iOS

January 6, 2020: Alas, as many people have pointed out, there is a bug with the dates if you have a start date other than January 1. That is due to the fact that 2020 is a leap year. A fix for this bug exists but the App Store review team keeps rejecting the update on the grounds that Yearly does not offer enough functionality according to Apple's ever-changing guidelines. I'm as frustrated as you are about this. Thanks for your patience while I build enough bells and whistles for Yearly to once again be complaint with Apple's guidelines.

Your data is safe and private. Yearly does not collect any data, either locally or in the cloud, except for the minimal settings that you specify in Yearly’s settings.
